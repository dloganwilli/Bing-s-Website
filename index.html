About Us

At Dirty Paw Kennel, we specialize in elite care and professional training for hunting and sporting dogs in the Big Timber, Montana area. Our mission is to develop well-rounded, reliable companions through proven methods and personalized attention. Our training program includes force fetch, water training, and consistent exposure to game birds to sharpen your dog’s instincts and discipline. With access to expansive acreage, your dog has the space and freedom to train in real-world conditions. We also offer convenient pickup and drop-off services to make the experience stress-free for both you and your dog. Whether you're preparing for the next hunt or want to elevate your dog’s performance, Dirty Paw Kennel delivers top-tier results with the care and expertise your dog deserves.
